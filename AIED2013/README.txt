The mobile version of Conference Navigator for EDM 2013, The 6th International Conference on Educational Data Mining and AIED 2013, 16th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Education. The app allows to view conference schedules, presented papers, workshops, tutorials, keynotes, and posters. You can mark and schedule papers you like to read or interested to attend. The schedule is synchronized with the Web version of the EDM x AIED 2013 Conference Navigator ( http://halley.exp.sis.pitt.edu/cn3/legacy.php?conferenceID=115 ).
