The mobile version of Conference Navigator for i-Know 2013, (http://http://i-know.tugraz.at/). The app allows to view conference schedule, presented papers, workshops, tutorials, and keynotes. You can mark and schedule papers you liked and interested to attend and the system will recommend more. Log in to your Conference Navigator account to synchronize the mobile app schedule with the Web version of the Conference Navigator (http://halley.exp.sis.pitt.edu/cn3/conferenceHomePage.php?conferenceID=126).
